**Climate-curated skincare from Canada.**

Atmosphera helps people and the planet flourish by designing skincare that builds resilience, respects nature, and empowers every person to find their element.

## Our mission

Founded in Canada a decade ago by esthetician **Katelyn Rousselle**, Atmosphera is built on over 20 years of hands-on experience treating clients' skin in dramatically different climates—from dry heat and high-altitude chill to coastal humidity and urban pollution. That experience led to a new category of skincare based on **climate typing**: the idea that your environment should guide the products you use.

Our formulas are designed to simplify routines and support the skin's natural resilience—skincare for real life, and for women who do it all.

## What we stand for

- **Expertise, real-world experience** — Performance-driven, climate-adaptive skincare that's as resilient as you are.
- **Where nature meets skin science** — Every formula is crafted in our Canadian laboratory with precise, climate-adaptive ingredients, tested to deliver visible results in any environment.
- **Uncomplicated. Powerful. Trusted.** — Effective formulas powered by active ingredients, always free from harmful additives:

  - Fragrance-free  
  - Paraben-free  
  - Cruelty-free  
  - Made in Canada  
  - Female-led
